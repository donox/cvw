# Welcome to Central Virginia Woodturners

This guide gives new members a quick orientation to the club and how we operate.

## Meetings

CVW meets on the **third Tuesday of each month**, 6:00 PM – 9:00 PM at:

> Crimora Community Center
> 1648 New Hope-Crimora Rd
> Crimora, VA 24431

A typical meeting includes a demonstration by an invited presenter or club member, Show and Tell, open gallery time, Q&A, and announcements.

## Getting Involved

- **Show and Tell** — bring a piece you've turned and share it with the group. All skill levels welcome.
- **Skill Center** — hands-on lathe time with mentoring from experienced turners. See the [Skill Center](/site/skill-center) page for details.
- **Volunteer roles** — the club depends on member volunteers. Talk to any officer if you'd like to help.

## Staying Connected

- Check the [Calendar](/site/calendar) for upcoming events.
- Watch for email announcements — make sure the club has your current email address.
- Past [Newsletters](/site/newsletters) are available on this site.

## Questions?

Reach out to any officer on the [Contact](/site/contact) page.
