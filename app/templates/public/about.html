About Central Virginia Woodturners

Who We Are

Central Virginia Woodturners (CVW) is a chapter of the American Association of Woodturners (AAW), based in the Richmond, Virginia area. Founded by a small group of enthusiasts, CVW has grown into a community of turners who range from complete beginners to nationally recognised artists.

We meet monthly for programs featuring demonstrations, gallery shows, and discussions. Members regularly share tips, tools, and timber from the meeting floor — and the friendly atmosphere makes every meeting a great place to learn.

Meetings

Frequency
Monthly — generally the third Saturday of each month
Time
9:00 AM – 12:00 PM
Location
Deep Run Park Recreation Center, Henrico County, Virginia
Format
Demonstration by invited presenter or club member, open gallery, Q&A, announcements

Guests are always welcome to attend a meeting before deciding to join. Membership applications are accepted at any meeting.
