Welcome to Central Virginia Woodturners

Central Virginia Woodturners (CVW) is a chapter of the American Association of Woodturners (AAW), serving turners of all skill levels in the greater Richmond, Virginia area. We meet monthly to share our passion for the art and craft of woodturning — from bowl turning and spindle work to artistic vessels and beyond.

Whether you are just discovering the lathe or have been turning for decades, you will find a welcoming community, excellent programs, and hands-on learning opportunities at CVW.
